approach

A literature review to answer the (sub-)research question(s) and formulate a framework and/or approach of product design management (the design (collaboration), distribution and personal fabrication process) to tackle the problem.
For this the research question need to be defined and additionally its sub-questions. These sub-questions will serve as guidelines to gain answers to specific challenges under the main research question. This information will then be analysed and contextualized into a design approach and/or framework.
The next step would be applying this framework/approach by creating the online platform, documentation and tools for designers to engage with the approach. These would need to be designed carefully:

  • What kind of tools are needed?
  • The documentation needs to be comprehensible and educational for a large range of experience in designers
  • The online platform needs to be approachable and accessible for designers, contributers, users and maybe easy customization.

In addition to this a testing approach should be setup in order to provide empirical basis for the framework/approach.
Because of the nature of this project it makes more sense to make students or people in the makes community use the approach to develop designs and the production documentation using the tools provided by this research. This would provide less bias in the resulting designs.
These designs can then be measured based on pre-defined factors like sustainability, accessibility, producability, and possibly creativity.
